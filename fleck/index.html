Questions

The short version.

Is Fleck therapy?

No. Fleck is a private journaling app. It is not medical care, therapy, crisis support, legal advice, or professional advice.

Do I have to write long entries?

No. Fleck is built for small voice notes. One sentence counts. Longer reflection is welcome when you want it.

What is a daily sketch?

A daily sketch is Fleck's journal entry assembled from the voice notes you left throughout the day. Think of it as a portrait painted from small flecks — each voice note adds a little color and shape.

How much does Fleck cost?

Fleck has a 7-day trial planned. After that, pricing is $3.99/month or $29/year.

Is my journal private?

Fleck is designed as a local-first journal. Some features, like transcription and AI sketches, may process text or audio with third-party services. The privacy policy explains what is processed and why.
