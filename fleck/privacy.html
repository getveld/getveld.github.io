Privacy Policy for Fleck: AI Journal

Effective date: May 15, 2026

Fleck is a voice-first journaling app. This page explains what information Fleck may process, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information Fleck may process

  • voice journal recordings created in the app
  • transcript text generated from recordings
  • journal-related text or context used to generate reflections or sketches
  • subscription and entitlement status related to Apple billing and RevenueCat
  • support communications if a user contacts support

How Fleck uses information

  • to let users record and revisit journal entries
  • to transcribe audio when transcription features are used
  • to generate reflections or sketches when those features are used
  • to manage subscriptions, restores, and entitlement status
  • to provide support if a user contacts us about a problem

Third-party services

  • Deepgram may process recorded audio for transcription
  • Google Gemini may process transcript text and related context for reflection or sketch generation
  • RevenueCat and Apple may process subscription, purchase, and restore information for billing and entitlement management

Data storage and privacy posture

Fleck is designed as a local-first journaling app. Some journal-related data may be stored on the user’s device. When transcription or AI reflection features are used, related content may be sent to the third-party services described above in order to provide those features.

Tracking and advertising

Fleck does not use ad tracking, IDFA, or ATT-based advertising flows based on the current app implementation.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need support, contact support@getveld.ai.
